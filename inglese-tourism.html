Sustainable Tourism and the 2030 Agenda

Why tourism matters to the Sustainable Development Goals, explained.

TopicSustainable tourism & the 2030 Agenda
SDGs directly named8, 12, 14
Also relevantSDG 10, SDG 11

Tourism represents roughly one in ten jobs worldwide and is explicitly named in three SDGs: SDG 8 (decent work), SDG 12 (responsible consumption) and SDG 14 (life below water, via target 14.7). SDG 10 and SDG 11 are also directly relevant: who actually benefits from tourism revenue, and whether cities remain livable for the people who live there year-round, not just for visitors passing through.

In sintesi

  • Tourism represents roughly one in ten jobs worldwide and is explicitly named in SDG 8, SDG 12 and SDG 14.
  • SDG 10 and SDG 11 matter too: who benefits from tourism revenue, and whether cities stay livable for residents.
  • Target 14.7 explicitly names tourism as a source of income for coastal and island economies.
